Tuna Melt Panini

Home

This tuna melt panini is crispy, cheesy, and packed with flavor. With
tangy pickle, melty American cheese, and a golden sourdough crunch, it's everything
you want in a hot sandwich.

Description

A tuna melt panini is one of those sandwiches that feels simple but totally delievers.
You mix canned albacore tuna with mayo, balsamic vinegar, chopped onion, and a bit
of dill pickle for a creamy, tangy filling. Combined with sourdough bread slices and
American cheese, the outside gets golden and crunchy. While the inside stays warm and gooey
with a tasty kick from the pickle and onion.

Ingredients

  • 1 (5 ounce) can solid white albacore tuna in water (such as Bumble Bee)
  • 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon chopped onion
  • 1 dill pickle, chopped
  • 4 slices sourdough bread
  • 2 slices American cheese

Steps

  1. Combine tuna, mayonnaise, balsamic vinegar, onion, and dill pickle in a bowl.

  2. Spread tuna mixture over 2 slices bread. Top with American cheese and
    remaining 2 slices bread.

  3. Preheat a panini press according to manufacturer's instructions.

  4. Cook in the preheated panini press until bread crispy and cheese
    melted, 3 to 4 minutes.
