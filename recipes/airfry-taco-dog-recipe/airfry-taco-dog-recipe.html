Airfried Taco-Dog

Home

Crispy on the outside, juicy in the middle! The airfried taco dog brings together
bold taco flavors and classic hotdog comfort. Wrapped, toasted, and topped with salsa, guac,
and jalapenos, it's a snack that doesn't mess around.

Description

A taco dog is what happens when you take a hotdog and give it a full-on taco makeover.
You start with a tortilla or bun, tuck in an airfried hotdog, and pile on all the good stuff!
In this recipe, that's salsa, guacamole, and jalapenos. You get the smoky and salty bite from the dog,
layered with freesh, spicy taco toppings that make every bite a little messy and very delicious.

Ingredients

  • 2 jumbo hot dogs
  • 1 teaspoon taco seasoning mix
  • 2 hot dog buns
  • 1/3 cup guacamole
  • 4 tablespoons salsa
  • 6 pickled jalapeno slices

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat an air fryer to 390 degrees F (200
    degrees C). Make sure the air fryer is at temperature for at least 4 minutes.

  2. Cut 5 slits into each hot dog. Evenly rub 1/2 teaspoon taco seasoning
    over each hot dog.

  3. Cook hot dogs in the air fryer basket for 5 minutes.

  4. Place hot dogs in buns and return to the basket. Cook until buns are
    toasted and hot dogs are crisp, and about 4 minutes more.

  5. Top hot dogs with equal amounts of guacamole, salsa, and
    jalapenos.
